Our Fellowship

Our Fellowship

Every Sunday, the Fellowship of Saints gathers to continue steadfastly in the apostles' doctrine and fellowship, as well as in the breaking of bread and prayers. (Acts 2:42)

Our Sunday services are generally held in English, but other languages may be available depending on the congregation.

The pattern of Worship:

  • Worship with Short Songs Exhortation for 20 minutes (sermon).
  • Individual adoration (Saints are encouraged to worship individually).
  • Before communion, a 40-minute exhortation (sermon).
  • Communion/Breaking of Bread.

At the start of the service, children remain with their parents or adults as we worship (sing songs) together for the first part of the morning. Someone will eventually announce that it is time for the younger members to go to their respective groups. You must accompany your children to their groups and register them by our child safety policy.

If necessary, there will be an opportunity for prayer following the conclusion of the main meeting.

Regular Bible studies, prayer meetings, fasting prayer meetings, and gospel outreaches are held.

The context for an in-depth, interactive study of God's word is provided by Bible Study. Our general practice is to study a book of the Bible or a theme from beginning to end over a period of several weeks. As always, we seek to comprehend its significance and relevance to each of our lives.

We pray for every member of our church and the world around us regularly during our prayer and fasting prayer meetings.

To spread the good news, we have regular gospel outreaches.

'Proclaim His glory among the nations, His wonders among all peoples,' says the Lord. Psalm 96:3

For more information, please contact us.

The Children's Ministry is awake and well, with Bible lessons, activities, games, and tons of fun!! Visual aids, cue cards, and experience-based examples are used to deliver lessons.

Our Sunday school teachers are well-versed in God's Word and base their ministry primarily on prayer. They don't just tell children Bible stories; they also teach them how to live their lives with Jesus. The lessons are more "Word" focused, preparing each of our children to "Remember your Creator in the days of your youth."

Aside from regular Sunday classes, our children enjoy Vacation Bible School and Summer Camps during the school holidays, which are held in various locations and provide a great opportunity for them to learn more about Jesus while also having fun together.

Our goal is to provide a safe and welcoming environment in which every child can be themselves. We want the kids to have a good time while learning about Jesus and meeting Him.

If you have any questions about the programs or would like to learn more about bringing your children on a Sunday, please fill out our Contact Us form.

Praise the Lord! Bethania House of Worship is a congregation founded on New Testament teachings. It is based in Garland, Texas, but has a global goal.It is a non-denominational, multi-cultural Bible church that follows the principles of both the Jerusalem church (Acts 2:42) and the Antioch church (Acts Chap. 11,12 & 13). We are a Christ-centered, Bible-based, family-oriented, evangelical church that exists to enable believers to worship the Lord in the splendour of Holiness.
