Welcome to Bethania House of Worship

KNOW, GROW, CONNECT AND SERVE WITH US

Praise the Lord! Bethania House of Worship is a congregation founded on New Testament teachings. It is based in Garland, Texas, but has a global goal.

It is a non-denominational, multi-cultural Bible church that follows the principles of both the Jerusalem church (Acts 2:42) and the Antioch church (Acts Chap. 11,12 & 13).

We are a Christ-centered, Bible-based, family-oriented, evangelical church that exists to enable believers to worship the Lord in the splendour of Holiness.

We believe in the Holy Bible's authority as the complete Word of God.

We believe in the Trinity, which is God's plan of salvation for humanity as planned by God the Father, carried out by God the Son through His atoning death on the cross and resurrection, and applied and worked out in the lives of believers by God the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the Universal Church, which is made up of all born-again believers all over the world, as well as the local expression of that body in every place where believers gather for worship, breaking of bread, fellowship, Bible study, and prayer.

We believe in the equality of believers in the body of Christ, that they are equally precious because they have been redeemed by the precious blood of Christ, and that they are equally important and useful because they have been indwelt by the Holy Spirit to contribute to the building up of the church, the spiritual house, and God's holy habitation to show forth His fullness, unity, love, wisdom, and power.

We believe in the Church's preparation for the Lord Jesus Christ's second coming, and worship is an important part of that preparation. As a result, we encourage believers to worship in spirit and truth around the Lord's table every Sunday, allowing them to declare the preciousness of the Lord as they have experienced it. We warn and encourage believers to take their rightful place at the Lord's table in order to be ready for the Lord's glorious appearing. This exercise is significant because the church is being sanctified and prepared as Christ's holy bride.

We believe in the church's stewardship in preaching the gospel to all peoples on a regular basis so that souls are saved and added to the church, discipled, and the kingdom of God is built. Every opportunity for the Gospel proclamation is taken, and every believer is encouraged to participate in the various outreach programs.

We believe in the operation of local churches under the headship and lordship of the Lord Jesus Christ, in accordance with the New Testament pattern, and in accordance with the principles revealed in God's word.
